Six Degrees of Bacon

  • Utilized AVL tree for rotations in combination with a shortest path algorithm.
  • Optimized ternary operators and tree size.
  • Coded in C++.

Six Degrees of Bacon is based off a widely popular game where any IMDB actor can be traced back to Kevin Bacon within 6 "degrees". Project aims to prove that through a shortest path algorithm.
